Frequently AskedQuestions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Replace your helmet if it's over five years old, damaged, fits poorly, or shows internal wear. Helmets degrade over time and older models might not meet safety standards.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Before riding your bike, check the tires, brakes, chain, and lights for any issues, and give the frame a quick once-over. A little prep can ensure a safe and smooth ride.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

Wear a helmet, follow traffic rules, stay visible, and ride on the left. Signal your turns, avoid distractions, and ride predictably to stay safe.
